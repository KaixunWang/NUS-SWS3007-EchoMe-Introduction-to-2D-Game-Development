# EchoMe

**Unity 3D Puzzle & Replay Game**

**Project Duration:** 2025-05-11 ~ 2025-07-21
**Location:** National University of Singapore(Summerwork shop SWS3007)

## About

EchoMe is a Unity-based 2d puzzle game developed during a summer internship at NUS.
The core idea is simple yet challenging: **record your actions and have a shadow replay them** to assist you in solving puzzles.

This project was as much about **learning Unity and gameplay programming** as it was about exploring game design and problem-solving under real-world constraints.

- **Game Proposal:** 2nd place
- **Prototype:** 1st place
- **Rough Game:** 2nd place
- **Alpha:** 2nd place
- **Beta Test:** 1st place
- **Final Game:** 1st place

This recognition reflects both the **quality of our work** and the **efficiency of our team collaboration** throughout the development process.

## Project Challenges

During development, we faced several technical and design challenges:

1. **Shadow Recording & Replay System:**
	- Ensuring the shadow accurately replayed the player’s actions in real-time.
	- Optimizing memory and performance to avoid frame drops while recording many actions.
2. **Puzzle & Level Design:**
	- Designing levels that were challenging but solvable using shadows.
	- Balancing difficulty across multiple playtests to ensure fun without frustration.
3. **Visual & Shader Effects:**
	- Creating ghostly shadow visuals with **Shader Graph** in URP.
	- Maintaining visual appeal while keeping the game performance-friendly.
4. **Team Collaboration & Time Management:**
	- Coordinating tasks and merging work from multiple team members efficiently.
	- Managing tight deadlines while iterating on mechanics, playtests, and feedback.
